Overview

Purpose

We at Wild Water Rafting have one purpose in mind, and that is to bring our customers all levels of acrenline inducing experiences safely to our customers at affordable prices.

Audience

Our primary audience is the young and the young at heart. We imagine primarily people in their 20's to 40's who are looking for a thrilling experience out on the water. We are prepared for all walks of life, whether it be a small knit group of friends, a large club, a couple or two, and even large family events. These people will want a fun day with little planning on their own parts, as time is a precious comedity. Do to this we offer shuttles, guides, and meals, along with gear necessary. Each of these are optional, making the entire experience completely customizeable, and budget friendly. My audience will likely view and place their order on a desktop or laptop, and will refer back to the schedule and other tips via mobile device. This is why we include options to enter a phone number for text reminders after tickets have been purchases in order to help our users stay effortlessely in the know. This will allow for quick sharing of information for everyone involved.
